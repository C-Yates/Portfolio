SolidWorks Design Automation

Originally this project started as an idea for standardisation of the product catalogue. Through a series of iterative loops, a set of manufacturing drawings act as a template for the products whilst the API makes changes to the content and exports the relevant data into excel and PDF documents.

During the process I use the API's native behaviour to dynamically generate new configurations to populate the 'templates'. The process repeats hundreds of times automatically in the background of the PC resulting in a standard range of products with the relevant documentation and technical drawings.
